遺傳愛畫畫媽媽的天賦，自幼美術課常拿高分，國中畢業曾報考復興美工，因自認缺乏創意沒去就讀，但我對影像熱情不減，專科畢業後，插大考取銘傳大學大眾傳播系，大三拍攝的戲劇短片，代表學校參加廣電比賽。
服役期間，在金門當新聞兵，退伍後當過攝影助理、遊戲編輯，最後在《蘋果日報》生根落腳，從事新聞工作十餘年，《蘋果》易主後曾轉換跑道做美術設計、中天新聞記者，目前是三立新聞網小夜主管。
面對大眾媒體轉型和自媒體興起，工作之餘持續進修，2020年完成輔大大眾傳播碩士學位，再到復興商工美工科念夜間部，加強手繪能力，復興畢業後，繼續留校旁聽廣告設計、室內設計課程，期望成為理論與實務並重的傳播人。Inheriting my mother's talent for drawing, I consistently scored high marks in art classes from a young age. After graduating from junior high school, I applied to Fu-Hsing Arts and Vocational High School, but declined due to a perceived lack of creativity. However, my passion for visual media remained undiminished. After graduating from junior college, I transferred to Ming Chuan University's Department of Mass Communication. In my junior year, I filmed a short drama that represented the school in a broadcasting competition.

During my military service, I served as a news soldier in Kinmen. After my discharge, I worked as a photography assistant and game editor before finally settling down at Apple Daily, where I worked in journalism for over ten years. After Apple Daily changed hands, I transitioned to art design and became a reporter for CTi News. Currently, I am the night shift supervisor for SET News Network.

Faced with the transformation of mass media and the rise of social media, I continued my studies in my spare time. In 2020, I completed a Master's degree in Mass Communication from Fu Jen Catholic University. I then pursued a nighttime degree in Art and Design at Fu-Hsing Commercial and Industrial Vocational High School to enhance my hand-drawing skills. After graduating from Fu-Hsing, I stayed on as an auditor for advertising design and interior design courses, hoping to become a communicator with both theoretical and practical expertise.
